Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from an individual's weight and height that is used to assess body fat and categorize weight status. Calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters (BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²)), this simple formula provides a reliable indicator of whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI serves as a useful screening tool for health professionals to identify potential weight-related health issues, such as heart disease, diabetes, and hypertension. To use the BMI calculator, simply input your weight and height into the designated fields, select your preferred height unit (centimeters or meters), and click the "Calculate BMI" button. The calculator will then display your BMI value along with a corresponding weight category, helping you understand your body composition and guiding you toward making informed health and lifestyle choices.
